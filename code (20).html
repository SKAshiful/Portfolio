Form No. 60 Template Design

This project features a meticulously crafted template for Form No. 60, designed using Microsoft Word. This form is essential for individuals who do not have a Permanent Account Number (PAN) and are entering specific financial transactions under Rule 114B of the Income Tax Rules. The template demonstrates attention to detail, accuracy in formatting, and adherence to professional standards required for official documentation.

Significance:
  1. Compliance with Regulations:
    The form is structured in alignment with legal requirements, ensuring accuracy and relevance for financial institutions and individuals.
  2. User-Friendly Layout:
    The layout is intuitive and clearly organized, with distinct sections for personal details, transaction information, and verification. This ensures ease of use for applicants and authorities.
  3. Professional Formatting:
    Every detail, including fonts, alignment, and spacing, has been carefully designed to reflect a professional and polished document.

Automation of Certificate Generation Using Mail Merge

This project demonstrates the use of the Mail Merge feature in Microsoft Word to automate the generation of certificates for multiple recipients efficiently. The certificates, like the Data Entry Training Program Certificate, are personalized with recipient details such as name, scores, and other relevant information.

Significance:
  1. Time Efficiency:
    The Mail Merge process automates certificate creation for a large number of individuals, significantly reducing the time and effort compared to manual entry.
  2. Seamless Integration:
    By linking a data source (such as an Excel sheet) containing recipient details with the certificate template, this process ensures accurate and error-free personalization.
  3. Customizable Output:
    Each certificate is generated with unique details while maintaining uniform formatting, ensuring a polished and cohesive final product.

Automated Salary Sheet

This is an Automated Salary Sheet that I created in Excel to calculate employee salaries efficiently. It includes essential salary components such as Basic Salary, House Rent Allowance (HRA), Dearness Allowance (DA), and Other Allowances, which together form the Gross Salary. The sheet also automatically calculates deductions like Provident Fund (PF) and Tax, ensuring accuracy in net salary calculations. This system simplifies payroll management by automating salary breakdowns based on predefined formulas.

Marksheet Automation

This Marksheet Automation project in Excel generates student marksheets based on data stored in the Data sheet, which includes student details, school name, subjects, and marks. The Marksheet sheet formats and displays the marksheet for an individual student, using their roll number for quick lookup. It calculates grades and remarks for each subject and checks if a student has failed more than one subject; if so, they are marked as Failed. Additionally, it calculates the overall percentage and final result, streamlining the marksheet generation process while ensuring accuracy.

Raja Ram Mohan Roy

This is my drawing of Raja Ram Mohan Roy, a tribute to the great reformer who played a crucial role in shaping modern India. I used DOMS color pencils to bring warmth and depth to his features, while Camel charcoal pencil helped enhance the contrast, especially in his expressive eyes and mustache. I paid special attention to the shading of his turban, blending green and yellow tones to add authenticity. The soft background keeps the focus on his resolute expression, reflecting his wisdom and determination. Through this artwork, I wanted to honor his legacy and inspire others to appreciate his contributions to society.
